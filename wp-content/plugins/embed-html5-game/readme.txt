Embed your HTML5 game, video, etc, on your WordPress site easily and make your HTML5 game or video fit on any screen size.

== Description ==

By using Embed HTML5 Game, you can embed your HTML5 game, video, etc, into your WordPress post, page or widget easily. Embed HTML5 Game can make your HTML5 game or video fit on any screen size.

Features:

* Your content will fit on any screen size.
* Shortcode generator.
* Easy to use.
* Add custom css.

== Installation ==

Upload the "Embed HTML5 Game" plugin to your blog, Activate it.
